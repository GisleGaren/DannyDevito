Me, Myself & I

This artwork was my first project ever, a portrait of myself. I loved doing this piece, adding the texture and colour, lights and shadows was fun, but at the same time difficult. When I started painting this piece, I didn't have any experience in art. Therefore this artwork is very special for me, and also a memorable moment because it was whilst painting that I understood my reason to be here and that I found my passion in my life. I also painted myself skinnier, which is why I love to paint, I get to paint whatever I want and how I want.

Late Night Vibes

This was my view from when I would sell hotdogs in the busy streets of New York. I would always dream about living in these amazing skyscrapers. After years of suffering and selling hotdogs and paint for hours I live in an apartment the size of a shoebox in Manhattan for 200 000 dollars a month. Life really is amazing, just keep believing and grinding and you will get there too.

Ocean Eyes

I met this boy in Africa, and his eyes were just drawn to me. His eyes reminded me of an ocean and I could spot them 1000 miles away. I kindly asked him if I could do a painting of him and he said no, therefore I stalked him for a week straight to memorize how his eyes looked. After that I asked for a date and sadly I was rejected, I think it was because of my height.
